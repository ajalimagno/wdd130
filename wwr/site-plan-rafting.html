Overview

Purpose

The rafting business aims to create an immersive and unforgettable adventure experience for visitors, offering a unique blend of thrill, education, and personalized service. By showcasing the beauty and majesty of the natural world, as well as the skills and knowledge required to navigate the wilderness, the rafting site will inspire and entertain customers while providing valuable lessons and experiences.

Audience

The target audience for a water rafting website is adults aged 18-65 who are interested in adventure, outdoor activities, and personal growth. Target customers have a moderate to high level of physical fitness and experience with water activities, and they have a diverse range of interests, such as whitewater rafting, kayaking, canoeing, or wildlife photography. Target customers are typically adults who are looking for a relaxing and enjoyable way to spend their leisure time, and they are willing to invest in a unique and memorable adventure experience. Target customers want to find a water rafting experience that is both thrilling and educational, as well as one that provides exceptional customer service. They expect to find information about the water rafting company, its history, and the team behind it, as well as easy booking and payment options, and the ability to customize their trips to their preferences. Target customers are likely to access the water rafting website through a desktop or laptop computer, as well as through a mobile device.
